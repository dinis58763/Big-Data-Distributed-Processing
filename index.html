Interstellar

Kinepolis, Bolesława Krzywoustego 72, 61-144 Poznań

"Interstellar" is a mind-bending science fiction epic directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a future where Earth faces a catastrophic agricultural crisis, a former NASA pilot, Cooper, embarks on a space mission to find a new habitable world. Accompanied by a team of scientists, Cooper traverses a wormhole near Saturn, encountering mind-bending phenomena and grappling with the concept of time. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the human spirit as Cooper's connection with his daughter, Murph, becomes a driving force in his quest to save humanity. With stunning visuals and a thought-provoking narrative, "Interstellar" takes audiences on a captivating and awe-inspiring journey through space and time.

Category: Science Fiction


The Notebook

Kinepolis, Bolesława Krzywoustego 72, 61-144 Poznań

"The Notebook" is a timeless romantic drama based on Nicholas Sparks' novel. Set in the 1940s, the film follows the passionate love story between Noah and Allie, two young individuals from different social backgrounds. Their summer romance faces obstacles such as disapproval from Allie's parents and the impact of World War II. Despite their separation, their love endures, and years later, an older Noah recounts their story to Allie, who suffers from dementia. The film explores themes of enduring love, fate, and the power of memories, capturing the hearts of audiences worldwide.

Category: Drama


Shrek

Kinepolis, Bolesława Krzywoustego 72, 61-144 Poznań

"Shrek" is an animated comedy film released in 2001, directed by Andrew Adamson and Vicky Jenson. The movie introduces us to Shrek, a lovable and grumpy ogre living in a swamp who is unexpectedly thrown into a quest to rescue Princess Fiona from a dragon-guarded tower. Shrek reluctantly teams up with Donkey, a talkative and enthusiastic donkey, to navigate through fairytale lands filled with quirky characters and hilarious situations. Along the way, Shrek and Fiona develop an unlikely bond, challenging traditional notions of beauty and true love. With its clever humor, memorable characters, and heartwarming message, "Shrek" has become a beloved animated classic for audiences of all ages.

Category: Animation
